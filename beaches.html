Beach Slatina

Opatija

The paved beach is surrounded by buildings and pines, has lounging areas, there are possibilities for renting sunshades and sun loungers, and there are also stairs with which they are easier to enter the sea. One narrow, artificial sandy beach with soft bottom and shallow coastline is very popular among small children.

Cafés and restaurants provide the pleasure of enjoying food and drink for adults, while children and younger will prefer to have fun on the playground or on jet skiing. Sanitary facilities, showers and changing cabins take care that nothing is missing on the beach. Due to the safety of guests there are also rescuers.

Beach Ičići

Ičići

The largest beach in Icici is so central and extensive, that you can reach it on foot. The pebbled beach is around 500 meters long and in some areas has partially cemented sunning areas dispersed in between the sand. This is the reason why you have such comfortable access to the water and a flat, gently sloping shore. The beach also has deserved its “Blue Flag”, keeping itself clean by providing facilities such as toilets and changing rooms.

This beach is popular with the young and the old and provides many opportunities for good food and leisure activities, such as water-skiing, jet-skiing and beach volleyball.

Beach Klančac

Brseč

Not far away from Bršeč, a small medieval Istrian town located on a high cliff offering a beautiful view of the Kvarner Bay and islands, are some of the most beautiful beaches in Istria, among which the beach Klančac is definitely one that needs to be mentioned. Hidden in a small picturesque bay and hidden by cliffs, this pebble beach is considered among the most popular on the Adriatic.

Even though arriving to the beach must be on foot, thanks to its exceptional beauty the beach Klančac attracts a large number of swimmers who enjoy the peace and quiet and want to spend the day at the sea at a safe distance from the characteristic tourist crowds.
